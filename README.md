This project showcases a professional food advertisement poster created in Adobe Photoshop. The design promotes two signature cakes—Strawberry Bliss Cake and Chocolate Decadence Cake—using attractive visuals, balanced composition, elegant typography, and persuasive marketing elements. The poster is designed to enhance brand appeal, highlight product features, and encourage customer engagement through a strong call-to-action. It demonstrates skills in graphic design, photo manipulation, typography, color harmony, and advertising layout creation.
